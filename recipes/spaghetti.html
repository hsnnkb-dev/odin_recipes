Spaghetti

Description

Our best ever spaghetti bolognese is super easy and a true Italian classic with a meaty, chilli sauce.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Mince
  • Carrots
  • Celery

Steps

  1. Boil Spaghetti
  2. Chop carrots and celery
  3. Cook Mince with carrots and celery
  4. Plate Mixture with Spaghetti
  5. Enjoy
